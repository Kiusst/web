What is tennis?

Tennis is a racquet sport played between two players (singles) or two teams of two players each (doubles). It originated in Europe in the late 19th century and has become a popular sport worldwide. The objective of the game is to hit a tennis ball with a racquet so that it passes over a net and lands within the boundaries of the opponent's court. Points are scored when the opponent is unable to return the ball correctly.

tennis benefits

  1. Cardiovascular health: Improves heart health and circulation.
  2. Physical fitness: Increases strength, endurance, flexibility, and coordination.
  3. Weight control: Helps burn calories and maintain a healthy weight.
  4. Mental health: Reduces stress and improves mood.
  5. Social interaction: Fosters relationships and teamwork.
  6. Discipline and concentration: Enhances focus and decision-making.
  7. For all ages: Suitable for people of all ages.
